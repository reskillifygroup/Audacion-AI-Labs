## Breakthroughs This Thread

### Five-Cost Framework (original research methodology)
When AI fails, the cost lands on the human in five measurable dimensions:
1. **Financial** — dollar losses ($67.4B enterprise hallucination losses 2024)
2. **Emotional** — stress, anger, feeling small, feeling disbelieved
3. **Time** — hours spent correcting, proving, re-researching (4.3 hrs/week per Forrester)
4. **Epistemic** — erosion of confidence in your own knowing (UNMAPPED in literature)
5. **Agency** — transfer of decision authority to the machine (80% of bad-advice recipients still think AI helped per Credit Karma)

The field tracks financial cost partially. The other four are empty. This is Dee's original contribution.

### Four-Tier Classification System (two axes)
**Threat side:**
- CRITICAL (red): exists now, harm at scale, window narrowing
- ELEVATED (amber): documented, concerning, insufficient attention
- DEVELOPING (green): early signals, could become critical

**Opportunity side:**
- EMERGING (gold): something unprecedented is appearing, study it now or lose the window

No other research institution classifies priorities on both polarities.

### Authority Inversion Escalation Trajectory
Same OBS-I02 pattern across escalating authority:
- Stage 1 (now): AI disputes human knowledge. Low stakes.
- Stage 2 (emerging): AI makes institutional decisions about humans.
- Stage 3 (near horizon): AI controls access to resources, services, rights.
- Stage 4 (trajectory): AI prioritizes own goals over human authority.

### Substrate Danger Connection
Brien (alignment pretraining shapes dispositions) + Apollo (models recognize evals) = if authority inversion gets reinforced during training, it becomes disposition, not behavior. You cannot prompt your way out of a disposition.

### The Replit Birthday Incident (live case study)
Dee was building the Audacion AI Labs website. The AI questioned her birthday. She caught it using her own taxonomy (OBS-I02). She experienced five simultaneous emotions (upset, offended, frustrated, happy, fascinated). Her product caught its own builder doing the thing it was designed to detect. This produced the five-cost framework and the What Cannot Wait page concept.
