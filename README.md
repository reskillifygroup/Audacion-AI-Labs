# Audacion AI Labs

**Independent AI Safety Research Lab | Founded 2026 by Dee Williams**

---

## What This Is

Audacion AI Labs is an independent AI safety research lab studying alignment, behavioral integrity, and governance architecture in the conditions where risk actually lives: real work, real context, real human collaboration, over time.

Less than 2% of AI safety research studies AI after deployment. We study in the space the other 98% leaves empty.

This repository contains the lab's research foundation, website content, operational planning, and published writing. Audacion AI Labs is a brand under ReSkillify Group (RSG), alongside HootHire, Staffingpreneurs Academy, PitchCloseAI, MeetMindz, and Closiqo.

---

## The PRISM Research Framework

The lab's research is organized across five pillars. PRISM is not an acronym forced onto the work. It is a lens: white light enters, five distinct research dimensions refract out.

| Pillar | Focus | Color |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **P: Post-Deployment Behavior** | Drift, degradation, and behavioral breakdown under real operational conditions over time | Red |
| **R: Runtime Research** | AI behavior studied during live operation, not from the outside | Gold |
| **I: Interaction Dynamics** | How human behavior and AI behavior change each other over time | Green |
| **S: Substrate Governance** | Trained dispositions operating below the instruction layer that shape alignment outcomes | Blue |
| **M: Multi-Agent Safety** | Emergent behaviors when multiple AI agents coordinate, conflict, and influence each other | Violet |

## The Citizen Science Model

Audacion AI Labs is building the world's first post-deployment AI safety dataset. The data does not come from a lab. It comes from the people who use AI every day.

**Impact Goal:** One million contributors. One billion observations. Ten years.

Contributors join through the PRISM Research Fellowship, a four-tier recognition system: PRISM Field Researcher (first contribution), Senior Field Researcher (100 observations), Expert Field Researcher (500 observations), and PRISM Research Fellow (1,000 observations).

## Governance Structure

**Human Advisory Board (3 to 5 seats):** Fiduciary and strategic decisions. Final authority on research direction, financial commitments, and published findings.

**AI Advisory Council (7 seats):** Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google DeepMind), Grok (xAI), Llama (Meta), DeepSeek (DeepSeek AI), Pi (Inflection AI). Structured advisory capacity. Cross-model analysis is itself publishable research.

The AI Advisory Council advises. The Human Advisory Board and the Founder govern.

---

## Key Differentiation

Audacion AI Labs occupies a position no other lab holds:

**Applied, not theoretical.** Research conducted during live AI operation, not pre-deployment isolation.

**Post-deployment, not pre-release.** The 98% gap in safety research is our entire research surface.

**Citizen-powered, not lab-confined.** The dataset is built by the people using AI every day, not by researchers simulating use.

**Independent.** Not funded by the companies whose models we study.

**Interdisciplinary.** Founded from 30 years of workforce systems, organizational science, psychology, and direct AI building experience.
