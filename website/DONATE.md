## WHERE THE MONEY GOES

### Every dollar has a job.

Audacion AI Labs is committed to transparency. We will publish an annual financial summary showing how donor funds are allocated across four categories:

**Research (target: 50% or more of all funds):** Researcher compensation, publication costs, peer review, data analysis, and research tools. Right now, this means building the field's first post-deployment AI behavior taxonomy (62 observable behaviors and growing, 15 of which exist in no published framework anywhere), conducting the longitudinal studies that reveal patterns invisible in any single session, and publishing findings that the entire field can use. The majority of every dollar goes here because this is why the lab exists.

**Technology (target: 20%):** Citizen science platform development, browser extension, API layer, data pipeline, and infrastructure. This is what makes one billion observations possible: the tools that let a teacher in Ohio or an engineer in Lagos report an AI behavior in 30 seconds and have that observation enter a five-layer research pipeline that transforms it into published science.

**Community (target: 15%):** PRISM Research Fellowship administration, Discord, events, contributor recognition, and outreach. The systems that connect one million contributors to each other and to the lab. This includes the citizen discovery attribution program where contributors who identify new AI behaviors are credited as discoverers, and the fellowship tiers that give the most engaged citizens deeper access to the research process.

**Operations (target: 15%):** Lab administration, legal, accounting, and organizational overhead.

We will never spend more on operations than on community. The research comes first. Always.

## GIVING LEVELS

### Observer ($1 to $49)
You see it. You fund it. Every observation matters, and so does every dollar that processes one. Observers receive a thank-you email and quarterly updates on what the research is producing.

### Researcher ($50 to $249)
You are funding the science directly. Researchers receive quarterly research briefings, early access to published findings, and recognition on our annual donor acknowledgment page.

### Fellow ($250 to $999)
You are funding the people who do the work. Fellows receive everything above plus an invitation to the annual PRISM Summit donor reception and a personal update from the founder twice a year.

### Architect ($1,000 to $4,999)
You are building the infrastructure. Architects receive everything above plus recognition in published research acknowledgments and a direct line to the research team for questions about the lab's direction and findings.

### Founder's Circle ($5,000+)
You are building the future alongside us. Founder's Circle members receive everything above plus a personal briefing with Dee Williams, naming recognition on a specific research output or initiative, and an invitation to an exclusive annual gathering with the lab's leadership and advisory board.

## TAX INFORMATION

Audacion AI Labs is a Delaware Public Benefit Corporation. Donations to a PBC are not currently tax-deductible under IRS rules. We are pursuing 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsorship to enable tax-deductible giving in the near future and will update this page as soon as that status is in place.

Until then, your contribution is a direct investment in independent AI safety research. We chose transparency over convenience. We will never tell you a donation is tax-deductible when it is not.
