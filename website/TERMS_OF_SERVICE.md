## 2. About Audacion AI Labs

Audacion AI Labs is an independent AI safety research lab founded in 2026 by Dee Williams. The lab conducts applied research across five dimensions of post-deployment AI safety under the PRISM Research Framework: Post-Deployment Behavior (P), Runtime Research (R), Interaction Dynamics (I), Substrate Governance (S), and Multi-Agent Safety (M).

Audacion AI Labs operates as a Public Benefit Corporation. Our public benefit purpose is to advance the science of post-deployment AI safety through applied research, citizen science, and open publication of findings.

Audacion AI Labs is not an AI product company. We do not develop, sell, or deploy artificial intelligence models. We study how AI systems behave after deployment, in real working conditions, with real human collaboration, over time.

## 6. PRISM Research Fellowship

The PRISM Research Fellowship is a four-tier recognition system for contributors who submit behavioral observations. The tiers are:

- PRISM Field Researcher (first contribution)
- Senior Field Researcher (100 observations)
- Expert Field Researcher (500 observations)
- PRISM Research Fellow (1,000 observations)

Fellowship tiers are verified digital credentials that may be displayed on professional profiles. They represent recognition of your contribution to post-deployment AI safety research. Fellowship designations:

- Do not constitute employment or an employer-employee relationship
