# Audacion AI Labs — Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information
## Effective Date: May 25, 2026 | Last Updated: May 25, 2026

---

## Our Commitment

Audacion AI Labs does not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. We do not share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.

This page is provided in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA, effective January 1, 2026). Under California law, "selling" includes disclosing personal information to a third party for monetary or other valuable consideration, and "sharing" includes disclosing personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes.

---

## What We Do With Your Data

**We use your data to operate the Website and conduct research.** Your account information, observation submissions, and usage data are used to provide the Services, maintain your account, operate the PRISM Research Fellowship, analyze aggregated research data, and improve the Website.

**We share data with service providers, not advertisers.** We share limited data with service providers who help us operate the Website (such as hosting providers, email platforms, and analytics tools). These service providers are contractually required to use your data only for the specific services they provide to us, not for their own purposes.

**We publish de-identified research data.** Observation submissions are de-identified (your name and personal details are removed) before they are included in published research datasets. De-identified data is not considered personal information under CCPA.

**We do not use marketing cookies at this time.** If we introduce marketing or targeting cookies in the future, we will update our Cookie Policy and provide an opt-out mechanism before activating them.

## Your Right to Opt Out

Even though we do not sell or share personal information as defined by CCPA, you have the right to submit an opt-out request at any time to ensure your data is protected as our practices evolve.

### How to Submit an Opt-Out Request

**Option 1: Email**
Send an email to privacy@audacionailabs.com with the subject line: "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"

Include your name and the email address associated with your account (if you have one). We will confirm receipt and process your request.

**Option 2: Phone**
Call (424) 999-0548 and request to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.

**Option 3: Global Privacy Control (GPC)**
Our Website honors Global Privacy Control signals. If your browser or device sends a GPC signal, we will automatically process your opt-out without requiring a separate request.

---

## What Happens After You Opt Out

After we process your opt-out request:

- Your personal information will not be sold or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes (this is already our practice, and your request ensures it remains so)
- Your account, observation submissions, fellowship status, and all other aspects of the Services will remain fully functional
- You will continue to receive transactional and service-related communications
- You will not be discriminated against for exercising your opt-out right

---

## Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit an opt-out request on your behalf. The authorized agent must provide proof of authorization (a signed written authorization or a power of attorney). We may contact you directly to verify the request.
