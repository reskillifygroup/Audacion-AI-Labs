## OPENING

Less than 2% of AI safety research studies AI in the conditions where risk actually lives. Audacion AI Labs was built to close that gap. We have the framework, the methodology, and a global citizen science model ready to deploy. What we need now are the partners and funders who understand that safe AI is not going to build itself.

---

## WHAT YOUR FUNDING ENABLES

Every dollar that enters Audacion AI Labs goes directly toward building the scientific foundation that AI governance currently lacks.

### The Research
Fund the studies that produce the missing science. Our PRISM framework covers five dimensions of AI safety that no other lab is studying together: post-deployment behavior, runtime conditions, human-AI interaction dynamics, substrate governance, and multi-agent safety. Your funding produces published papers, open frameworks, and findings the entire field can build on.

### The Tool
Fund the development of the citizen science platform that collects post-deployment behavioral data from real AI users around the world. A browser extension. An API layer. A reporting interface. This is the infrastructure that makes one billion observations possible.

### The People
Fund the researchers, fellows, and staff who turn raw observations into real science and real solutions. Analysts who find the patterns. Researchers who publish the findings. Engineers who build the tools. Policy minds who translate the science into governance that organizations and governments can actually use. Every hire accelerates the path from a field observation to a safer AI system.

### The Community
Fund the community infrastructure that connects one million contributors to each other and to the lab. Discord. Events. Recognition programs. The PRISM Research Fellowship. A movement requires more than a mission. It requires a home.

## WHY THIS LAB

There are hundreds of AI safety organizations. Here is why this one is different.

We don't study AI in isolation. We study it where it works, where it breaks, and where it interacts with humans over time. No other lab occupies this position.

We don't keep the research behind closed doors. Our findings are open. Our dataset is governed. The science we produce belongs to the field.

We don't recruit from one discipline. Our contributors span every industry, every country, and every level of AI experience. The dataset we build is the most diverse post-deployment behavioral dataset in existence because the people who build it are.

And we don't wait for a crisis to study safety. We build the science before the governance needs it, so that when policy catches up, there is something real underneath it.

Fund specific research projects, tool development, or fellowship programs. We are actively applying for NSF SBIR, Cartier Women's Initiative, CalOSBA Innovation Grants, and other aligned funding programs. Your grant accelerates our timeline.

Partner with us as a university research collaborator, a corporate research sponsor, or an institutional ally. We welcome partnerships that expand the lab's reach without compromising its independence.

Sponsor the PRISM Research Fellowship program, community events, or specific research publications. Your brand aligns with the future of AI safety.
