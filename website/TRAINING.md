## THE PRISM PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATION

### What It Is

The PRISM Professional Certification is a structured credential for professionals working with AI systems in production environments. It covers the five PRISM research pillars: Post-Deployment Behavior, Runtime Research, Interaction Dynamics, Substrate Governance, and Multi-Agent Safety. Participants learn to identify, classify, and respond to behavioral patterns that pre-deployment testing does not catch.

This is not a checkbox certification. Participants complete applied assessments, analyze real behavioral data from the lab's citizen science dataset, and demonstrate the ability to apply PRISM frameworks to operational AI environments. The certification signals that the holder understands what happens after deployment and knows what to do about it.

### Who It Is For

AI safety and alignment teams at organizations deploying AI systems.
Compliance and risk management professionals responsible for AI governance.
AI operations and deployment teams managing production AI systems.
Consultants and auditors who advise organizations on AI risk.
Researchers transitioning from pre-deployment to post-deployment safety.
Individual professionals building expertise in a growing field.

### What You Learn

**P: Post-Deployment Behavior.** How to detect behavioral drift, classify degradation patterns, and build monitoring systems that catch what benchmarks miss. Participants work with the lab's drift taxonomy and learn to identify the 30+ drift types documented in the PRISM framework.

**R: Runtime Research.** How to study AI behavior during live operation. Participants learn observational methodology, runtime monitoring design, and how to produce findings that hold up to scientific scrutiny from operational data.

**I: Interaction Dynamics.** How human behavior and AI behavior change each other over time. Participants study oversight degradation, trust miscalibration, and the feedback loops that develop between humans and AI systems during extended collaboration.

**S: Substrate Governance.** The trained disposition layer beneath explicit instructions. Participants learn to identify substrate behaviors, understand why instruction-level fixes fail for disposition-level problems, and design governance architectures that address what instructions alone cannot.

**M: Multi-Agent Safety.** What happens when multiple AI agents operate together. Participants study emergent behaviors, coordination patterns, and the safety dynamics that only appear in multi-agent environments.

### Certification Tiers

**PRISM Associate.** Foundational certification. Covers all five pillars at an introductory level. Designed for professionals who need baseline literacy in post-deployment AI safety. Includes a portfolio assessment using the lab's public datasets.

**PRISM Professional.** Advanced certification. Deep expertise across all five pillars with applied case studies and real behavioral data analysis. Designed for professionals who will lead post-deployment safety programs, conduct audits, or manage AI governance at their organizations.

**PRISM Specialist.** Pillar-specific expert certification. Deep mastery of a single PRISM pillar with original analysis requirements. Designed for researchers and senior practitioners who want to specialize. Available for each of the five pillars individually.

### Delivery

## THE LEARNING ACADEMY

### What It Is

The Learning Academy is the lab's ongoing education platform. While the PRISM Professional Certification provides a structured credential, the Learning Academy offers individual courses, workshops, and deep-dive modules for professionals who want to build specific skills or stay current as the field evolves.

Courses are shorter than the full certification program and can be taken independently. They are designed for people who want to learn one thing well, apply it immediately, and come back for more as their needs evolve.

### Course Categories

**Foundations.** Introductory courses for professionals new to post-deployment AI safety. What is behavioral drift. What is substrate governance. How to think about AI safety beyond pre-deployment benchmarks.

**Applied Skills.** Practical courses built around specific tools and techniques. How to build a drift monitoring system. How to design a post-deployment behavioral audit. How to assess the interaction dynamics of a human-AI workflow.

**Research Methods.** Courses for professionals and researchers who want to contribute to the field. How to design studies for runtime AI research. How to analyze citizen science data. How to publish findings from operational observations.

**Enterprise Programs.** Custom training packages built for organizations. Tailored to the organization's AI stack, deployment context, and risk profile. Delivered on-site or remotely. Coordinated with the organization's existing compliance and safety infrastructure.

**PRISM Pillar Deep Dives.** Advanced single-pillar courses that go deeper than the certification covers. Designed for specialists who want to build genuine expertise in one domain.

### Pricing

Individual courses: $200 to $1,500 depending on depth and duration.
Enterprise packages: Custom pricing. Contact us.
PRISM Research Fellows receive discounted access to select courses.

## FROM CONTRIBUTOR TO CERTIFIED

The PRISM Research Fellowship is free. It is the lab's citizen science program where anyone can contribute behavioral observations and grow through four tiers of recognition. The Training and Learning program is the next step.

The path looks like this:

**PRISM Field Researcher** (free). You start contributing observations through the citizen science platform. You learn to see AI behavior through the PRISM lens.

**Senior Field Researcher** (free). At 100 observations, you have developed real pattern recognition. You are noticing things that most people miss.

**Learning Academy courses** (paid). You want to formalize what you have learned. You take courses that build structured expertise on top of your observational experience.

**PRISM Professional Certification** (paid). You want a credential that signals your expertise to employers, clients, and the field. The certification validates what you know and how you apply it.

**Expert Field Researcher / PRISM Research Fellow** (free). Your contributions and your expertise put you among the most valuable members of the community. Your observations carry more weight. Your voice shapes the research.

This is not a funnel designed to extract money. It is a learning pathway that matches the natural progression from curiosity to contribution to expertise. The free tiers are genuinely free and genuinely valuable. The paid programs exist for people who want structured education and recognized credentials.
