## HEADLINE

Partner with the lab that studies what happens after deployment.

---

## OPENING

Audacion AI Labs produces post-deployment AI safety research that no other lab is producing. Corporate Research Partners get structured access to those findings before they are published, along with direct engagement with the research team, priority access to evaluations, and a seat at the table where the science is being built.

If your organization deploys AI systems, this partnership gives you what pre-deployment benchmarks cannot: ongoing intelligence on how AI actually behaves in production, where governance gaps emerge, and what risks the field has not yet addressed.

---

## WHAT PARTNERS GET

### Research Access
Early access to research findings across all five PRISM pillars before public release. Quarterly research briefings with Pillar Leads. Annual research summary customized to your industry and deployment context.

### Evaluations and Assessments
Priority scheduling and partner pricing for Post-Deployment Safety Audits and Regulatory Readiness Assessments. Your organization's AI systems evaluated using the PRISM framework by the team that built it.

### PRISM Summit and Events
Reserved seats and speaking opportunities at the annual PRISM Summit. Invitations to roundtables, think tanks, and retreats throughout the year. Visibility to the AI safety community as an organization that takes post-deployment safety seriously.

### Training and Certification
Discounted cohort pricing for the PRISM Professional Certification. Enterprise training packages tailored to your team's roles and deployment context.

### Advisory Engagement
Quarterly advisory calls with the research team. Input into the lab's research agenda on topics relevant to your organization's deployment challenges. Access to cross-model analysis and the AI Advisory Council's structured responses to your research questions.

### Brand and Credibility
Listed as a Corporate Research Partner on the lab's website and in publications. Co-branding opportunities at events and in public communications. Association with the leading independent post-deployment AI safety research lab.

---

## PARTNERSHIP TIERS

### Research Partner
Annual membership providing research access, quarterly briefings, PRISM Summit reserved seats, and partner pricing on evaluations and training. Designed for organizations that want ongoing intelligence on post-deployment AI safety.

### Strategic Partner
Everything in Research Partner, plus advisory engagement, co-branding opportunities, input on research direction, and priority access to the lab's full service catalog. Designed for organizations that want a deeper relationship with the lab's research program.

### Founding Partner (limited)
The highest tier of engagement. Everything in Strategic Partner, plus named recognition as a founding supporter, a permanent seat at the annual research advisory roundtable, and direct engagement with the Founder and CSO. Available to the first organizations that commit to a multi-year partnership. Limited to 10 Founding Partners.

### Pricing
Research Partner: $25,000 per year.
Strategic Partner: $50,000 per year.
Founding Partner: $100,000+ per year (multi-year commitment).

Custom partnership structures are available for organizations with specific needs. Contact us.
