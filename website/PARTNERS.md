## OPENING

Audacion AI Labs produces post-deployment AI safety research that no other lab is producing. Corporate Research Partners receive quarterly briefings where the research team translates published findings into actionable intelligence for their specific industry, deployment context, and governance needs. Partners get evaluations, training, advisory engagement, and a seat at the table where the science is being applied.

If your organization deploys AI systems, this partnership gives you what pre-deployment benchmarks cannot: ongoing intelligence on how AI actually behaves in production, where governance gaps emerge, and what risks the field has not yet addressed.

**Our independence is non-negotiable.** Partners receive access, briefings, and advisory engagement. Partners do not see findings before publication. Partners do not influence research direction, methodology, or publication decisions. This firewall is not a policy. It is architecture. It protects the integrity of the science and, by extension, the value of every partnership built on top of it.

---

## WHAT PARTNERS GET

### Research Access
Quarterly research briefings with Pillar Leads who walk your team through published findings and what they mean for your specific deployment context. Annual research summary customized to your industry and governance needs. Access to the full catalog of published research, frameworks, and open datasets.

### Evaluations and Assessments
Priority scheduling and partner pricing for Post-Deployment Safety Audits and Regulatory Readiness Assessments. Your organization's AI systems evaluated using the PRISM framework by the team that built it.

### PRISM Summit and Events
Reserved seats and speaking opportunities at the annual PRISM Summit. Invitations to roundtables, think tanks, and retreats throughout the year. Visibility to the AI safety community as an organization that takes post-deployment safety seriously.

### Training and Certification
Discounted cohort pricing for the PRISM Professional Certification. Enterprise training packages tailored to your team's roles and deployment context.

### Advisory Engagement
Quarterly advisory calls with the research team. Bring your deployment challenges and get structured guidance grounded in the lab's published research. Access to cross-model analysis and the AI Advisory Council's structured responses to your research questions.

### Recognition
Listed as a Corporate Research Partner on the lab's website and in publications. Association with the leading independent post-deployment AI safety research lab. Your partnership signals to your industry, your regulators, and your customers that your organization takes post-deployment AI safety seriously.

### Data Partnership
Provide access to your AI systems or anonymized deployment data for the lab to study. Data partnerships produce research findings specific to your platform's behavioral patterns in post-deployment conditions. The lab publishes its findings independently regardless of what they show. Data partnerships are governed by the same editorial firewall as every other partnership: access does not create influence.

## PARTNERSHIP TIERS

### Research Partner
Annual membership providing research access, quarterly briefings, PRISM Summit reserved seats, and partner pricing on evaluations and training. Designed for organizations that want ongoing intelligence on post-deployment AI safety.

### Strategic Partner
Everything in Research Partner, plus advisory engagement, recognition in publications, priority access to the lab's full service catalog, and data partnership eligibility. Designed for organizations that want a deeper relationship with the lab's research program.

### Founding Partner (limited)
The highest tier of engagement. Everything in Strategic Partner, plus named recognition as a founding supporter, a reserved seat at the annual research advisory roundtable for the duration of the partnership, and direct engagement with the Founder and CSO. Available to the first organizations that commit to a multi-year partnership. Limited to 10 Founding Partners.

### Pricing (founding-year rates)
Research Partner: $25,000 per year.
Strategic Partner: $50,000 per year.
Founding Partner: $100,000+ per year (multi-year commitment).
