## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website operators information about how the site is being used.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use on audacionailabs.com (the "Website"), why we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. This Policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which provides full details about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

---

## Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, account authentication, session management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of strictly necessary cookies because the Website cannot function without them.

**Purpose:** Account login, session management, security protections (CSRF), load balancing, accessibility preferences

**Duration:** Session (deleted when you close your browser) or Persistent (up to 1 year)

**Third Parties:** None. Strictly necessary cookies are first-party only.

### Functional/Preference Cookies

These cookies remember choices you make to improve your experience, such as your display name preferences, language settings, or theme preferences (if applicable). They do not track your browsing activity on other websites.

**Purpose:** Remembering your settings and preferences, maintaining your display choices

**Duration:** Persistent, up to 1 year

**Third Parties:** None currently.

### Analytics/Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Website by collecting information about which pages are visited, how long visitors spend on pages, and how they navigate the site. This information is aggregated and anonymized, meaning it cannot be used to identify you personally.

**Purpose:** Website traffic analysis, user behavior patterns, identifying areas for improvement

**Duration:** Persistent, up to 2 years

**Third Parties:** Google Analytics. Google Analytics uses cookies including _ga and _gid to distinguish unique users. Google's privacy policy governs Google's use of the data collected. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on (https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Marketing/Targeting Cookies

We do not currently use marketing or targeting cookies. If we introduce marketing cookies in the future, we will update this Cookie Policy and provide notice before activating them. Marketing cookies would only be placed with your explicit consent.

## How to Manage Cookies

### Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. The following links may help you manage cookies in common browsers:

Google Chrome: chrome://settings/cookies
Mozilla Firefox: about:preferences#privacy
Safari: Preferences > Privacy
Microsoft Edge: edge://settings/content/cookies

Please note that if you disable or delete cookies, some features of the Website may not function properly. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled because they are required for the Website to operate.

### Opt-Out Preference Signals

Our Website honors Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals and other opt-out preference signals recognized under CCPA/CPRA (effective January 1, 2026). When your browser or device sends a valid opt-out signal, we will:

- Disable non-essential cookies (analytics and marketing)
- Process your opt-out of any sale or sharing of personal information
- Display confirmation that your opt-out request has been honored

### Do Not Track (DNT)

Our Website responds to Do Not Track signals. When a valid DNT signal is detected, we will disable non-essential cookies and analytics tracking for your session.
